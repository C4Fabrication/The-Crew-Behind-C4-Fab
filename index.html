The Importance of Individualized Care

Wether you’re an avid off-roader or the weekend adventurer, we are all looking for the same thing, right? What is that you might ask? Freedom!

So what’s freedom to you?

Here at C4, this is how we define freedom...

Staring down the edge of a 1000 ft cliff, feeling the wind rush against your face.Setting up camp after 100 miles of kidney splitting off road trails, while you take in the smell of brats and whiskey around the campfire.Feeling the chill of that brisk mountain air at 13,000 ft, while snuggling into a warm sleeping bag for the night.Squaring up to the lip of that double with the 50ft gap, feeling the tightness in your chest as you grip the steering wheel.Inhaling the smell of the summer air that’s filled with the smell of gasoline and burnt rubber.

These are the feeling of exhilaration, the adrenaline that makes you feel alive.The experiences our culture longs for at the end of a long hard week.The desire to escape our mundane lives and go explore the unknown.

We know the type, because we are the type!

We enjoy the outdoors, we enjoy adventure, we enjoy the heart stopping thrill and the new experiences, friendships and inspiration that come from it. So that’s why we have created a product that seamlessly embodies all of those. We bring precision to inspiration, and provide the protection you need with the aggressive styling you want.

What's next for C4?

Last year was our biggest year yet, we produced more products than we had ever thought possible and moved into a new building more than 4x the size of our last building. Our team is now close to 30 people and we are working our hardest to get everyones products out the door as quickly as possible.

Going in to 2022 we are focused on lowering our lead times and continuing to produce the highest quality products in the Toyota Off Road/Overland industry. We believe that our customers want their products to look just as good as they perform. With that in mind the design team at C4 strives to create the best balance of both. We are so excited for this next year and we have a ton planned for the year, such as: new product releases, giveaways and attending a lot more events. We are starting this blog to connect with our customers on a deeper level and share our expertise in the off road industry. We will be posting a blog post once a month where we will talk about all thing off roading and give you guys a more personal glimpse into the crew behind C4!
